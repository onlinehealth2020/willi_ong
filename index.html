Tropical Chicken and Pineapple Salad

Description

Ingredients 3 cups cooked chicken (diced) 1 1/2 cups celery (chopped) 1/2 cup pineapple (crushed, drained) 1/2 cup mayonnaise (or more, as needed) Salt to taste Black pepper to taste 8 leaves lettuce (or more as desired) Optional: 2 hard-cooked eggs (sliced)

Cooking

1

This chicken salad is made with the addition of crushed pineapple, some celery, and optional hard-cooked eggs. It's a simple salad with an uncomplicated list of ingredients.

2

The recipe calls for crushed pineapple, but add some cubed pineapple if you'd like more texture. Or serve it along with sliced pineapple. Add avocado wedges on the side and garnish the salad with some sliced green onion tops or chopped cilantro for extra color.

3

Steps to Make It In a serving bowl, combine the diced chicken, celery, and pineapple. Add 1/2 cup of mayonnaise and toss gently. Add more mayonnaise, as needed. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve with lettuce and garnish with sliced egg, if desired.
